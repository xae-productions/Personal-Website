Mountains Over Beaches

Fresh Mountain Air And Breathtaking Views Await You On Our Hiking Trails

Would you rather have a mountain dayhike or a beach trip? Your choice actually says a lot about you.

A study compared geographic preference with personality types. It showed that introverts perfer mountains. Meanwhile, extroverts like to do their socializing on wide, open spaces such as the beach. No wonder the best parties occur here!

Why are mountains better?

The incredible picturesque landscapes, clean fresh air, peaceful and tranquil; the mountains are full of unmatched beauty. They offer you the chance to disconnect and escape from the hustle and bustle of life.

What activities can you do on the mountains?

There are numerous activities you can participate while being in the mountains. From parachuting to snowboarding to even caving and rafting. Whether you are searching for an adrenaline rush or just a calming experience, there are activities for all to enjoy.
